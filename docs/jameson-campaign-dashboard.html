Key Learnings & Recommendations

Strategic insights across both Jameson campaigns — Fall Sports & St. Patrick's Day

Fall Sports Takeaways

🏆
Above-Benchmark ROAS
Both Display and OLV exceeded category benchmarks — Jameson's targeting strategy during sports moments is highly effective at driving measurable purchase behavior.
📺
Display vs. OLV Efficiency
46.3M impressions delivered across both formats. Display drove stronger direct ROAS while OLV supported brand building — a complementary strategy for sports seasons.
💡
Recommendation: Scale Fall Sports
With $1.08M in traceable sales and 11% sales lift, fall sports is a proven performance driver. Consider increasing investment for NFL playoffs and bowl season.

St. Patrick's Day Takeaways

☘️
34% In-Store Lift is Exceptional
A +34.04% featured items lift during SPD confirms Jameson's cultural relevance and the power of WMC targeting to drive shelf pull during this critical holiday window.
🎯
Display Standard: Star Channel
Display Standard delivered $3.72 ROAS vs. $0.65 for OLV Standard. Reallocating 20-30% of OLV budget to Display Standard could significantly increase overall campaign ROI.
👥
40% New Buyer Rate = Long-Term Value
10,571 new buyers acquired at $193.7K in new buyer sales. These first-time Jameson purchasers at Walmart represent compounding LTV if retargeted throughout the year.
