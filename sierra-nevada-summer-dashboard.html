What is Rest of Market?

WMC campaigns generate sales not just at Walmart — shoppers exposed to Walmart Connect ads also buy at other retailers like Target, Kroger, and convenience stores.

NCS (Nielsen Catalina Solutions) measures these off-Walmart purchases by comparing exposed vs. unexposed shopper groups across the entire market.

Sierra Nevada's ROM iROAS of $4.08 is significantly above industry benchmarks — every $1 invested in WMC drives $4.08 in incremental sales at other retailers.

🔮 Recommendations

Future Considerations

Strategic priorities informed by Summer 2025 campaign learnings

📅

Always-On Strategy

Shift from seasonal bursts to always-on media with seasonal heavy-ups around key grilling & craft beer occasions.

📺

CTV + Audio Depth

Deeper investment in CTV and Audio to amplify ROM lift — these upper-funnel tactics drive the $4.08 ROM iROAS.

🎯

Occasion-Based Creative

Cross-category targeting with occasion-based creative (grilling, camping, BBQ) to capture contextual demand.

🚫

Exclusionary Audiences

Suppress heavy buyers from upper-funnel campaigns to reduce waste and focus budget on conquest & new buyers.

🎨

Creative Variation Testing

Test different creative messaging by tactic — conversion-focused for Display/Native, awareness for CTV/Audio.

📈

Scale Incrementally

With a proven $4.52 combined iROAS, incremental investment has strong justification — every $100K added returns ~$452K.
