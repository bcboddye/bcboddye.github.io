Strategic Recommendations

Based on Q4 2024 and Q4 2025 campaign learnings

🎯
Scale Recent Buyers Targeting

Recent Buyers used only 4% of spend but drove 19% of sales — the highest-efficiency audience segment by far. Allocate more budget here first.

🔄
Retarget New Buyers

Q4 2024 acquired 43.6K new buyers with an estimated $548K in additional 12-month return value at a 17.3% repeat rate. Retarget Q4 2025's 57.3K new buyers.

🏆
Conquest the Category

89.1% of in-market whiskey buyers chose a competitor. A dedicated conquesting strategy targeting category buyers could meaningfully shift share.

📦
Lean Into Gift Sets

Gift Set co-purchase frequency in Q4 2025 signals strong gifting intent. Create Q4 creative specifically for the holiday gift-giver mindset.

📱
Invest in Online/OPD

Q4 2025 Online Sales Lift hit +68.63% (vs. 8.46% in 2024). This dramatic growth signals digital demand is accelerating — increase digital investment.

🌍
Leverage ROM Halo Effect

Q4 2024 NCS data showed $7.38M incremental at non-Walmart retailers — a massive halo effect. Walmart DSP drives broader market growth, not just Walmart.com.
